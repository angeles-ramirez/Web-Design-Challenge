Weatherpy Project

This website shows change in weather as you move closer to the equater. This project is based on weather data from over 500 different cities. The cities were chose at random and varied in distances from the equator. The data was pulled from the OpenWeatherMap API, and the cities were processed with their city number and city name. Python on Jupyter Notebook, along with Matplotlib and Citipy, were used to visualize the weather of all the cities.

With the data collected, I was able to build a series of scatter plots to showcase the followign relationships:
  • Temperature (Farenheit) vs. Latitude
  • Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
  • Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude
  • Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude
