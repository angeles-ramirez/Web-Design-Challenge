City Latitude vs Max Temperature

This graph was created using Matplotlib, and the scatter plot showcases the correlation between a city's latitude and it's highest recorded temperature. The take-away from this graph is that a city's latitude is an indicator of how high of a temperature a city's temperature may rise.
